The Competition

The Baja SAE Competition challenges student teams worldwide to demonstrate engineering innovation, technical expertise, and resilience. Each season, our team designs, builds, and tests a custom off-road vehicle, aiming to excel in performance, durability, and teamwork.

Learn more

The Engineers

Driven by passion and creativity, our engineers bring together a diverse range of skills to design, build, and optimize our vehicle. Each member contributes vital expertise, whether in 3D modeling, CNC machining, or system integration, to turn our shared vision into a Baja-ready machine.
